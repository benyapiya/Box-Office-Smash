About Project

Prediction: How much revenue will a particular movie gross in sales?The entertainment industry is a lucrative sector. In particular, the film industry is responsible for providing accessible and affordable entertainment to viewers. Determining which factors contribute to the success of a movie is important to its investors and to the entertainment industry as a whole. While some movies generate billions of dollars in box office sales, others lack in viewership and movie profits.

Thus, our group is interested in examining the features and characteristics that make a movie successful. Such features and characteristics include examining the movies’ genre, its release date, actors/actresses, the directors, critic responses/reviews about the movie, producers, the movie studio and the success rate of a movie’s franchise. To predict how a particular movie will gross in sales, we will analyze previous performance of movies and develop a model to forecast a movie’s revenue.

Team members: Joel Pepera, Radha Yadavilli, Victoria Anorve, Benya Piyathamsoontorn & Dan Zhou
